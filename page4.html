Step 1:
When you spawn in

When you spawn, go up to the villager on the far right and right click him.

Step 2:
Finding the portal

Once you spawn again, just in front of you, on the left hand side, you will see the corridor.

Just walk towards the glass and you will be teleported to the Stream SMP.

Troubleshooting Steps

Are you sure Jonah is streaming on Stream SMP?

The Stream SMP is not open 24/7 (although the server is), make sure Jonah is streaming on it.

Are you seeing an error come up in chat?

Please open a ticket on the Minecraft Discord asap!

It's still not working...

Join our Minecraft Discord and open a ticket. The staff team will try and resolve your problem as quickly as possible.
