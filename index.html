FAQ

How do I join the server?

Take a look at the navigation bar at the top of the site and click on "How to join".

Can I join on mobile/console?

Unfortunately, we do not have a, maintained, bedrock-edition server at the moment.

Can I stream/record on the server?

Of course! Feel free to make content on the server. All we ask you to do is, follow the rules and place the server IP or the URL of this website in the description.

What are server ranks?                                 

Server ranks is a way that you can directly support the running of the server (it's expensive)! Depending on which rank you buy, you can gain access to private worlds, receive in-game perks, and more!
