How to join Stream SMP
How to join the world Jonah streams on

Have you made it onto the server but don't know how to join the "Stream SMP" world? Take a look at these steps.

Click Here
Step 1:
The multiplayer page

Once you've clicked on the Multiplayer tab, go to the bottom of the page and click on "Add Server".

Step 2:
Adding a server

Fill in the fields with the same text you can see on the left.

Then click "Done".

Step 3:
Joining a server

Now, find "jonahwill.org" in the list of servers, click on it once, and then click "Join Server".

If an error comes up, try going through the steps below.

Troubleshooting Steps

What version are you on?

Make sure you are on Java 1.16.4 or 1.16.5 (both work). You can join using optifine etc. but not with Forge.

Have you restarted your game?

Many weird errors can be resolved by restarting your game and your launcher. Simply close Minecraft and your launcher (if open) and try joining again.

It's still not working...

Join our Minecraft Discord and open a ticket. The staff team will try and resolve your problem as quickly as possible.
