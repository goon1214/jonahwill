Useful Links

A list of useful websites/links, they're not that important but nice to have.

Staff Home
Plan

Here you can find all the stats you could pretty much think of. Most importantly, it has a player list with a search bar. You can find a player and look how active they are etc. (useful for assessing bans).

Colour Codes

Do you want to make your messages or (some) signs look extra fancy? Here you can find colour codes for use within the game. You can also use &#<code> to use hex colours (any colour you can think of).

Netdata

For those of you that really want to get into the specifics of the server's performance, you can use this website to see the node's and servers' performance.
